Writing Tips

Essential guides to help you write better essays and express your ideas clearly.

🧱

Essay Structure

  • Introduction: Hook → Background → Thesis
  • Body Paragraph 1: Topic sentence → Evidence → Explanation
  • Body Paragraph 2: New point → Support → Link
  • Conclusion: Restate thesis → Summary → Final thought
🔗

Linking Words

  • Adding: Furthermore, In addition, Moreover
  • Contrasting: However, On the other hand, Although
  • Concluding: In conclusion, To sum up, Overall
  • Example: For instance, For example, Such as
✅

Writing Checklist

  • Did you write a clear thesis statement?
  • Does each paragraph have one main idea?
  • Did you use linking words to connect ideas?
  • Did you check your grammar and spelling?
  • Is your conclusion different from the introduction?
📝

Useful Phrases

  • "In my opinion, …"
  • "It is widely believed that …"
  • "This shows that …"
  • "To illustrate this point, …"
  • "Taking everything into account, …"
⏳

Using Tenses

  • Present Simple: facts & habits — "Water boils at 100°C"
  • Present Continuous: now — "I am writing an essay"
  • Past Simple: completed — "They invented the sundial"
  • Passive Voice: processes — "The clock was used to track time"
🌟

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • ❌ Don't start sentences with "Because"
  • ❌ Don't repeat the same word too often
  • ❌ Don't mix past and present tenses
  • ✅ Use synonyms to vary your vocabulary
  • ✅ Read your essay aloud before submitting

Time Traveler's Essay Challenge
